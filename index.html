What we do

Warm support, tailored to your family

Every MilaClan service is non-medical and shaped around a personalized care plan. Here are the ways we help older adults stay comfortable, connected, and safe at home.

Companion Care

Friendly company, conversation, and day-to-day support to keep life social and connected at home.

Personal Care

Respectful help with bathing, dressing, grooming, and daily routines that supports independence and comfort — from trained, certified caregivers.

Homemaker & Household

Meal preparation, light housekeeping and laundry, errands, and help keeping the home running smoothly.

Dementia Companionship

Patient, structured companionship and engagement for memory loss, with a calm and familiar routine.

Respite Care

Scheduled relief so family caregivers can rest and recharge, while a loved one stays in good hands.

Overnight Care

Awake or asleep overnight presence for safety, comfort, and peace of mind.

Medication Reminders

Friendly prompts and reminders to help with self-administered medication. We do not administer medication.

Transportation

Rides to appointments, errands, and community outings in a safe, comfortable vehicle.

Not sure which fits? We'll help you choose during a free in-home consultation. Care is priced to your plan — contact us for a personalized quote.

Our story

Inspired by family

MilaClan is a family-owned, Washington State–licensed home care agency serving families across the Seattle Eastside. We believe growing older at home should feel safe, dignified, and warm.

Our care is culturally attuned — we respect each family's language, customs, and traditions — and built around genuine relationships between clients and caregivers. We focus on the things that make daily life easier and more connected, always within a non-medical scope.

  • Dignity and respect in every visit
  • Consistent caregivers, chosen for the right fit
  • Clear, caring communication with families
